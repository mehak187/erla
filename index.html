Specialised Biosafety Solution

ERLA Technologies (M) Sdn. Bhd. ("ERLA") was founded in Malaysia in 1995 to service the scientific community in the Biosafety and Life Science Industry. ERLA is the pioneer in the manufacturing of Biosafety Cabinets and Laminar Airflow Cabinets since 1995 in Malaysia. During the Nipah Virus Outbreak in Malaysia in 1998, ERLA's expertise were sought by the government agencies to understand the engineering of Biosafety and implementing the same on a laboratory scale. ERLA has a team of experts with a combined experience of more than 50 years in the Biosafety and Clean Air Technology field.
