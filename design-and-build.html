Design & Build Projects

ERLA specializes in the design, construction and certification of Biosafety Laboratories Levels 1, 2, 3 & 4 for research and educational institutes, as well as for the medical and pharmaceutical industries. ERLA's expertise also includes the feasibility and master planning of projects, as well as the design and fabrication of mechanical, electrical, structural and HVAC systems, all in line with current internationally accepted standards and best practices.
