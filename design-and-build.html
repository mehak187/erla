Design & Build

ERLA Technologies (“ERLA”) principal activity is in the designing, constructing and certifying of laboratories for research institutions, educational institutions and the medical and pharmaceutical industries. ERLA specializes in the construction of bio-infectious containment facilities at the P1, P2 and P3 levels. ERLA has completed projects which involve the highest and most stringent standards in existence today, which were implemented and adhered to when completing the design and construction of the National Public Health Laboratory in Sungai Buloh, Selangor, Malaysia Which is a bio-safety level 3 laboratory capable of containing the SARS, Nipah virus and the H5N1 avian flu virus.
