Our Services

ERLA provides Planned Preventive Maintenance (PPM) and Scheduled Corrective Maintenance (SCM) for:

  • Laminar Air Flow Systems
  • Biological safety Cabinets
  • Cytotoxic Drug Safety Cabinets
  • Clean Rooms
  • Biosafety Laboratories
  • Fume Hoods

ERLA's team of technicians are trained in the United States of America and Australia, and have provided testing, servicing and certification in South East Asia and the Middle East. Our team are specialized in testing and certification of Laminar Air Flow Cabinets, Cytotoxic Drug Safety Cabinets, Fume Hoods, Biological Safety Cabinets, Clean Rooms and Biological Safety Laboratories.

ERLA tests and certifies using the Australian Standards AS1807.
